Single Vendor E-Commerce Website

The e-commerce website will include functionalities such as product listing, shopping cart, checkout process, user authentication, payment gateway integration, order management, and user profile management.

Features

Product Functions
  • User registration and login
  • Product browsing and search
  • Shopping cart management
  • Order placement and payment processing
  • Order tracking
  • User profile managemen
  • Admin dashboard for product and order management
User Registration and Login
  • Users shall be register using email and password.
  • Users shall receive an email confirmation upon successful registration.
  • Users shall be able to log in using their email and password.
Product Management
  • Admin users can add, edit, and delete products.
  • Each product has a title, description, price, SKU, and images.
  • Users shall be able to browse and search for products.
Shopping Cart
  • Users shall be able to add products to the shopping cart.
  • Users shall provide shipping and billing information during checkout.
  • The system shall integrate with third-party payment gateways for payment processing.
Checkout and Payment
  • Users can proceed to checkout from the shopping cart.
  • Users provide shipping and billing information during checkout.
  • The system integrates with third-party payment gateways for payment processing.
  • Users receive an order confirmation email after successful payment.
  • Users shall receive an order confirmation email after successful payment.
Order Management
  • Users shall be able to view their order history and status.
  • Admin users shall be able to manage and update order statuses (e.g., processing, shipped, delivered).
User Profile Management
  • Users shall be able to view and update their profile information.
  • Users shall be able to manage their shipping addresses and payment methods.
Admin Dashboard
  • Admin users shall have access to a dashboard to manage products, orders, and users.
  • The dashboard shall provide analytics and reports on sales and user activity.
